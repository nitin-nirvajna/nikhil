About Me

The Story Behind the Byline

I'm a media professional with over a decade of editorial leadership across India's top digital newsrooms. My work sits at the intersection of rigorous journalism and digital strategy — making complex science accessible, making election nights comprehensible, and making every headline count.

My niche is science and space reporting — from ISRO's missions to NASA's deep-space discoveries — delivered in both English and Kannada to audiences who hunger for real insight. I launched the popular weekly series 'Vishwa Kannota' (World Wonder) at Times Internet, building a loyal readership around astronomy and aerospace.

I believe great journalism is built on clarity, speed, and trust — and that AI tools, used thoughtfully, can sharpen each of those without compromising editorial integrity.
